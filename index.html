What is a community hostel?

A community hostel is a locally owned accommodation that goes beyond just providing a bed. It's embedded in its neighborhood, employs local staff, hosts cultural programming like cooking nights, music sessions, and walking tours, and creates authentic connections between travelers and local culture. Unlike standard hostels that optimize for price or party, community hostels optimize for local experience, community, and belonging.
